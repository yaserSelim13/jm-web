How JoinMe Works

JoinMe helps you meet people nearby through small, authentic activities. Join something spontaneous, create a simple plan, chat only as much as needed, and turn online interest into a real-life moment.

📍 Spontaneous

Some of the best moments happen without a plan. Turn on Spontaneous Mode to see people nearby who are free right now - for coffee, sports, a walk, studying, partying, or anything simple and real.

📅 Planned

Have an idea for later? Create a small activity and let others nearby join. Keep it casual, choose the time and place, and make your week feel more alive.

✅ Safe & Flexible

Stay in control of how you meet. See the activity details, decide who joins your plans, and use chat to coordinate without getting stuck in endless messages. Real-life connection stays the focus.
