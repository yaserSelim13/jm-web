Safety Standards

JoinMe helps people connect, but each user is responsible for their own decisions and safety during meetups.

1. User Responsibility

Every user is responsible for their own behavior, choices, and personal safety when using the app or meeting others.

2. Reporting

Users can report content or other users in the app. Reports are acknowledged automatically.

3. Manual Review

Reports are reviewed manually by the app operator. Based on that review, content or accounts may be removed.

4. Safety Tips

  • 👥 Meet in public places like cafes or parks.
  • 📱 Let a friend or family member know your plans.
  • 🕒 Meet at a suitable time.
  • 🚗 Use your own transport or public traffic.
  • ⚠️ Trust your instincts and leave if something feels off.
  • 🚨 You can always report another user in the app.

5. Contact Information

JoinMe provides tools and guidance, but users remain responsible for their own safety.

To report abuse, email us at: support@join-me-app.com
