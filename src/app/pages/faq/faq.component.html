FAQ

What is JoinMe?

JoinMe is a social app for spontaneous, real-life activities with people nearby. No big events, no complicated planning - just small meetups that happen naturally.

What is spontaneous mode?

Spontaneous Mode shows people nearby who are free right now and ready to do something. Share what you feel like doing, see who's around, chat briefly, and meet in real life.

What is planned mode?

Planned Mode lets you create a small activity for the next few days. People nearby can discover it, join, and meet up when the time comes.

Are matches guaranteed in a short time?

No. JoinMe is about real people and real moments, so matches are never guaranteed. When it clicks, great. When it doesn't, it can still be a genuine experience.

Can I use spontaneous mode everywhere and anytime?

Technically yes. But since we are still small, it may not be useful everywhere. We publish weekly meetup time-windows in specific Berlin areas, and we update them in the app.

Do I have to pay to use JoinMe?

No. We just started, so JoinMe is free right now. We currently use ads and may add paid features later.

Do I have to share my location?

Not always. But in spontaneous mode, location is required to find people nearby. Please use this mode in public places and disable location when you are not in the mood to meet.
